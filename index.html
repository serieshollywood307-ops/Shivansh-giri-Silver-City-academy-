About Us

Welcome to Silver City Academy

Located in Gola Gokarnath, Lakhimpur Kheri, Silver City Academy is dedicated to fostering an environment where students are inspired to reach their full potential.

Our school offers a dynamic and nurturing atmosphere where academic excellence, personal growth, and creativity come together. With passionate educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to holistic learning, we equip students for success.
